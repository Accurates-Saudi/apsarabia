About Us

Accurate Project Solutions (APS) is a trusted industrial and project solutions company based in Saudi Arabia, delivering high-quality products, equipment, and comprehensive supply solutions across multiple industries.

We specialize in sourcing and supplying reliable industrial materials, safety products, tools, and essential consumables to support operations in oil & gas, construction, manufacturing, and infrastructure sectors.

With a strong commitment to quality, efficiency, and customer satisfaction, APS serves as a dependable partner for businesses seeking consistent and cost-effective trading solutions. We work closely with clients to understand project requirements and deliver fit-for-purpose products on time. Our responsive support team ensures smooth coordination from inquiry through final delivery.

Vision

To be a leading industrial and project solutions partner in the GCC, recognized for reliability, quality, and excellence in service.

Mission

To deliver high-quality industrial products and trading solutions through strong supplier networks, efficient operations, and a customer-focused approach.
