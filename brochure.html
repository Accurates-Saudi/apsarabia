About Us

Industrial & project solutions · Saudi Arabia

APS is a trusted industrial and project solutions company based in Saudi Arabia, delivering high-quality products, equipment, and comprehensive supply solutions across multiple industries.

We specialize in sourcing and supplying reliable industrial materials, safety products, tools, and essential consumables to support operations in oil & gas, construction, manufacturing, and infrastructure sectors.

With a strong commitment to quality, efficiency, and customer satisfaction, APS serves as a dependable partner for businesses seeking consistent and cost-effective trading solutions. We work closely with clients to understand project requirements and deliver fit-for-purpose products on time. Our responsive support team ensures smooth coordination from inquiry through final delivery.

Vision

To be a trusted regional supplier of oil and gas plant materials, project supply solutions, and industrial consumables, recognized for reliability, quality, and timely delivery.

02

Mission

To support construction, maintenance, and operations with dependable materials that meet industry standards, while providing competitive pricing, efficient service, and consistent client support.
